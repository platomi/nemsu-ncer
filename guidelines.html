CALL FOR PAPERS

  NCER 2021 welcomes and accepts scientific contributions on the presentation of original research papers in any of the following tracks:

  1. Pedagogical Approaches and Innovation
  2. Science Technology and mathematics Education
  3. Language Education
  4. Technology Education
  5. Multicultural Education
  6. Business Management Education

  Interested participants who presented their papers may wish to publish on the official research journal of North Eastern Mindanao State University, the SDSSU Multidisciplinary Research Journal (SMJR).

Abstract Submission:

1. Submitted abstract should be in English.
2. The research should be an original output in 1 of the aforementioned tracks.
3. Abstracts should be submitted on or before November 5, 2021, the last day of submission following the prescribed format:

Title. The title of the paper should be centered, in all capital letters, type written in bold, 11 pts, Arial and arranged in an inverted pyramid. The title must accurately describe the research paper and must not contain abbreviations, proprietary names, chemical formulas, outdated terminologies and jargons.

Author(s) . The author's full name are typewritten in bold, lower case except the first letter, and placed below the title. It should follow the format: First name, Middle initial, Last name, (Suffix). An asterisk (*) should be affixed before the name of the presenting author and a superscripted “+” sign on the name of the author of correspondence. E-mail address and other contact information (mobile number) of the author of correspondence should be written below the author name(s). Complete name and address of the office/institution of all authors should also be specified. In cases where there are several authors coming from different agency/institution, use superscripted numbers (after the author name/s) to indicate the author and corresponding address.

Abstract . The abstract is typed as a single-paragraph and must represent the content of all main sections of the paper. It must briefly and clearly discuss the main objective and the scope of the research, describe the methodology used, and summarize the main findings and significance. The abstract must not exceed 250 words and should not contain tables, figures and references.

Keywords . These are written below the ABSTRACT with a maximum number of 5 keywords or short phrases that can be used to index the research paper.


Scientific Papers:

Competing scientific papers for the Best Paper Award is required to submit the full paper following, but not limited to, the specified format below:

1. The manuscript should be in English, typewritten double-spaced in a letter size (8.5” x 11”) bond paper, using 11-point Arial font. The margin should be 1.5 inches on the left-hand side and 1 inch on top, bottom and right sides of bond paper. Page number should appear on the upper right-hand corner of the page. The paper should not exceed 16 pages, including Figures and Tables.
2. The sequence of contents of the manuscript - The manuscript should be organized in the following order:

Title page. Full title of the paper should be centered, all capital letters, typewritten in bold, 11 pts Arial and arranged in an inverted pyramid. The title must accurately describe the research paper and must not contain abbreviations, proprietary names, chemical formulas, outdated terminologies and jargons. Author(s) full name are typewritten in bold, lower case except the first letter, and placed below the title. It should follow the format: First name, Middle initial, Last name, (Suffix). An asterisk (*) should be affixed before the name of the presenting author and a superscripted “+” sign on the name of the author of correspondence. E-mail address and other contact information (mobile number) of the author of correspondence should be written below the author name(s). Complete name and address of the office/institution of all authors should also be specified. In cases where there are several authors coming from different agency/institution, use superscripted numbers (after the author name/s) to indicate the author and corresponding address.

Abstract. The abstract is typed as a single-paragraph and must represent the content of all main sections of the paper. It must briefly and clearly discuss the main objective and the scope of the research, describe the methodology used, and summarize the main findings and significance. The abstract must not exceed 250 words and should not contain tables, figures and references.

Keywords. These are written below the ABSTRACT with a maximum number of 5 keywords or short phrases that can be used to index the research paper.

Text. The text follows the ABSTRACT with main headings in the following order: INTRODUCTION, MATERIALS AND METHODS, RESULTS AND DISCUSSION, CONCLUSION, ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS and REFERENCES. The REFERENCES must follow the APA 7th edition. Main headings should be centered with all letters capitalized and set in bold. Secondary headings, if any, should not be indented, still in bold letters having the first letter of each word capitalized. Tertiary headings, if any, should be underlined, with only the first letter of the first word capitalized.


Oral Presentation:

1. Oral Presentation will be done on December 4-5, 2021 via zoom conferencing platform.
2. Each presenter will be given a total of fifteen minutes, ten minutes is allotted to the presentation of the salient points of the paper, and five minutes for clarificatory questions for the panel of adjudicators. The allotted time must be observed properly. The moderator will interrupt the presentation if the presenter exceeded in time.
3. The presenter must use 2003 MS powerpoint version document during the presentation with a maximum of 20 slides, for a 10-minute presentation. Slides must be readable from at least 10 feet.
4. Only one presenter is allowed during the presentation. The presenter must be in corporate attire.
5. The panel of adjudicators may ask clarificatory questions to the presenter after the oral presentation.
6. The structure of the oral presentation should only be limited to the following:

Brief Introduction which must set the context, help define the problem and its significance and present the hypothesis/es

Methodology which must be concised, use flow chart/schematic diagram is desired.

Results and Discussion which must include critical analysis/evaluation of evidence.

Conclusion and Recommendation.

7. The names of the authors and the institution represented must be placed at the end of the presentation.
8. The medium of communication is English except for necessary scientific names or vernacular findings which should have English translations.
9. A Certificate of Recognition will be given to the researcher/s at the end of the convention. Each plenary will declare Best in Oral Presentation (First, Second and Third).
